Regional Traffic DeepNet Models

ConvLSTM (NIPS-15) [3]:

It extends LSTM to have convolutional structures to better capture spatio-temporal correlations.

  • Paper: Convolutional LSTM Network: A Machine Learning Approach for Precipitation Nowcasting.

DeepST (SIGSPATIAL-16) [4]:

The first deep learning-based prediction model for grid-based spatio-Temporal data.

  • Paper: Deep Spatio-Temporal Residual Networks for Citywide Crowd Flows Prediction

ST-ResNet (AAAI-17) [5]:

A ResNet-based method, which shows promising results on citywide crowd flows prediction.

  • Paper: Deep Spatio-Temporal Residual Networks for Citywide Crowd Flows Prediction.

ST-3DNet (TITS-19) [6]:

It uses 3D convolution to capture the correlation of traffic data in both spatial and temporal dimensions.

  • Paper: Deep Spatial-Temporal 3D Convolutional Neural Networks for Traffic Data Forecasting

STDN (AAAI-19) [7]:

It employs CNNs and LSTMs to capture spatial and temporal correlations separately, and an attention mechanism to model long-term periodic temporal shifting.

  • Paper: Revisiting Spatial-Temporal Similarity: A Deep Learning Framework for Traffic Prediction.

DeepSTN+ (AAAI-19) [8]:

The state-of-the-art method for urban flow prediction, which can capture long-term spatial dependencies as well as the effect of land functions.

  • Paper: DeepSTN+: Context-Aware Spatial-Temporal Neural Network for Crowd Flow Prediction in Metropolis.
